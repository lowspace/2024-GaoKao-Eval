   - A. $\frac{x^2}{16} + \frac{y^2}{4} = 1 (y > 0)$
   - B. $\frac{x^2}{16} + \frac{y^2}{8} = 1 (y > 0)$
   - C. $\frac{y^2}{16} + \frac{x^2}{4} = 1 (y > 0)$
   - D. $\frac{y^2}{16} + \frac{x^2}{8} = 1 (y > 0)$
